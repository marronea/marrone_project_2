CI


CI has always been a go to for me. I’ve had a lot of good times here and it will always hold a little place in my heart. The prices are good for broke college students like myself and the atmosphere is fairly casual. My roommates and I will often stop in to play some rounds of pool on the weekdays.

Like the other bars with basements, I’m not a huge fan of this one either. It’s not as dark as some of the other basements but it can smell awful.


Anthony Marrone
